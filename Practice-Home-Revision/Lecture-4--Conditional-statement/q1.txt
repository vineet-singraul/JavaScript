### ✅ 1. **Button click par text "Hello" ko "Hi" me badlo**

* **Question**: Agar `<p>Hello</p>` hai, toh button click par use "Hi" karna hai. Agar already "Hi" hai toh "Hello" karna hai.
* **Output**:

  * Pehle: `Hello`
  * Button click: `Hi`
  * Dobara click: `Hello`

---

### ✅ 2. **Age input do, agar 18 ya usse zyada ho toh "Allowed" dikhao, warna "Not Allowed"**

* **Question**: User se age lo aur check karo kya wo 18+ hai?
* **Output**:

  * Input: `17` → Result: `Not Allowed`
  * Input: `18` → Result: `Allowed`

### ✅ 3. **Password input lo, agar sahi ho toh "Correct Password" warna "Wrong Password"**

### ✅ 4. **Light on/off text toggle karo**

* **Question**: Ek button click se text "Light is ON" ya "Light is OFF" me toggle ho.
